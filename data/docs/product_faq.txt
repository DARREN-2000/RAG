ACME SaaS Platform — Product FAQ
==================================

1. What is ACME SaaS Platform?
ACME SaaS Platform is a cloud-based project management and team collaboration tool designed for small and medium-sized businesses. It provides task tracking, file sharing, time logging, and real-time messaging in a single workspace.

2. How do I create an account?
Visit https://acme.example.com/signup. Enter your work email address, choose a password, and click "Create Account". You will receive a verification email within a few minutes. Click the link in that email to activate your account.

3. What pricing plans are available?
- Free Plan: Up to 5 users, 2 projects, 1 GB storage.
- Starter Plan ($12/user/month): Unlimited projects, 10 GB storage, email support.
- Pro Plan ($29/user/month): Everything in Starter plus priority support, advanced analytics, and 100 GB storage.
- Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing. Contact sales@acme.example.com for a quote.

4. How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Go to Settings → Billing → Change Plan. Select the desired plan and confirm. Changes take effect immediately; billing is prorated for the current period.

5. Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes. You can cancel from Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. We do not offer refunds for partial months.

6. Is there a free trial?
Yes. Every new account starts with a 14-day free trial of the Pro Plan. No credit card is required to start the trial.

7. What file types are supported for upload?
You can upload any file type (PDF, DOCX, PNG, MP4, ZIP, etc.). Individual file size is limited to 100 MB on Starter and 500 MB on Pro and Enterprise plans.

8. Does ACME support single sign-on (SSO)?
SSO via SAML 2.0 is available on the Enterprise plan. Contact our support team to configure SSO for your organization.

9. What integrations are available?
ACME integrates with Slack, Microsoft Teams, Google Drive, Dropbox, GitHub, Jira, and Zapier. Additional integrations can be built using our REST API.

10. How do I invite team members?
Navigate to Settings → Team → Invite Members. Enter the email addresses of your team members and click "Send Invitations". Invitees will receive an email with a link to join your workspace.
